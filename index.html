A Little Story About Me.
what we do?


Saara Apparel, known best for its quality, has carved a niche in the garment industry in a very short span of time with its presence for more than a decade in the industry. The company is empowered by a team of qualified professionals who leave no stone unturned to meet the demands of all our customers in this world of fashion. Each and every working procedure is inspected by our team of experts to ensure high quality and on time delivery. Specialized in manufacturing hish fashion women garment on a wide range of fabrics like cotton, silk, viscose, polyster, linens etc., Saara presents an elegant range of fabrics to women who prefer a strong sense of design and style.

Infrastructure

We are integrated unit having production facility to manufacture both woven and knits garment with in house computerized and manual embroidery machines enabling us to supply apparels at the best price, with best quality and on time delivery.After, years of dedication and perseverance, we have strengthened the areas of production, research and technology in our industry, to satisfy our customer's demands and desire.We have developed a unique integrated task coordination system, safety management and quality check operations schedule coordination system to accommodate smooth running conditions. Saara is committed to the guiding principles of quality, in time delivery, fair price and integrity, which has helped reach the great height of success.We have a team of well experienced employees who undertake quality checks, finishing and on time deliveries on daily routine basis and have a proven record of many years in driving heaps of customers towards our company.

Products

To keep up with the latest and perfect fashion styles for every season on all occasion, we are constantly adding new ladies apparel to our selection of tops, bottoms, dresses, blouses,t-shirts, kurthi, skirts, legging and jeggings.We are engaged in manufacturing all types of garments on a variety of fabrics bases such as chiffons, crepe, georgette, twills, high twist and many more.Saara offers a wide variety of specialized women wear fabric both imported and indian.
